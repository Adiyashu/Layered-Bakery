Our Philosophy

Not a commercial chain. A custom cake studio.

Welcome to Layered With Love, located in Chitragupta Nagar. Founded in 2015, we moved away from the mass-production bakery model to focus entirely on quality, detail, and taste.

As a delivery-focused home bakery, every cake is baked strictly to order. From gluten-free options to elaborate fondant artistry, we tailor every detail to your celebration.
