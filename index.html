Our Philosophy

Not a commercial chain. A custom cake studio.

Welcome to Layered With Love, located in Chitragupta Nagar, Patna. Founded in 2015, we specialize in made-to-order custom cakes and specialty desserts with a focus on quality, detail, and taste.

As a delivery-focused home bakery, every order is freshly baked — no pre-made cakes, no shortcuts. We offer eggless, sugar-free, and gluten-free options for every celebration.

Simple Process

How to Order

Ordering your custom cake is easy. Just follow these steps:

1

Share Your Idea

Send us your cake design, reference photo, flavor preference, and occasion details via WhatsApp or call.

2

Get a Quote

We'll confirm the design, size, price, and delivery time. Custom designs need 24-72 hours advance notice.

3

Confirm & Pay

Confirm your order with 50% advance payment via UPI, Google Pay, PhonePe, or bank transfer.

4

Fresh Delivery

Your cake is baked fresh and delivered to your doorstep in Patna. Pay the remaining amount on delivery.
